Welcome to Kitty Coffee the meow meow cafe

Kitty Coffee is a cozy and charming cafe that opened in 2023 in the heart of Barcelona.

Located on Passeig de Gràcia, Kitty Coffee offers a variety of delicious drinks and pastries, as well as a friendly and welcoming atmosphere. You can choose from a range of coffee options, from espresso to cappuccino, or try one of their special blends, such as the Kitty Latte or the Mocha Meow.

Whether you are looking for a relaxing break, a quick bite, or a meeting place with friends.

Come and discover why Kitty Coffee is the cat's meow!
